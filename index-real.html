    about the award

    HH Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai launched on the 24th of June 2009 the Emirates Government Excellence Award under the umbrella of the SKGEP to be the highest award of corporate excellence in UAE, aiming to promote the role of the federal government institutions serving the UAE society at all levels.

General Framework of Sheikh Khalifa Government Excellence Program
SKGEP seeks to achieve the perceptive vision of His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan and his Deputy His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashed Al Maktoum, Vice President, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai. This vision aspires to enable the UAE public sector to excel in its systems, performance, services and outcomes.
