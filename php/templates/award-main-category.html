Emirates award
main categories

The main category is divided into three awards. Application to these awards is obligatory if the applying entity fulfills the requirements in terms of administrative hierarchy (Regions or offices under a certain entity).

main categories

This category includes all government entities and all entities to be established in the future under certain decrees. There is a separate award for ministries and other authorities. The Award adopts international concepts and principles of corporate excellence as well as the EFQM criteria for the assessment of institutions and measurement of their performance level. The Award aims to create a transformational change and a comprehensive improvement of performance of federal ministries and government institutions.
