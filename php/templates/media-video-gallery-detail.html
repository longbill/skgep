HH Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai

April 4, 2013

{{HH Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai launched on the 24th of June 2009 the Emirates Government Excellence Award under the umbrella of the SKGEP to be the highest award of corporate excellence in UAE, aiming to promote the role of the federal government institutions serving the UAE society at all levels.}}

{{The Emirates Government Excellence Award consists of five categories; the main categories, in which awards are presented for an entity, region and department, based on the grand total score of the excellence model criteria. Awards of sub-categories are presented to the best entity achieving the highest results according to each separate criterion in the excellence model. Best practices categories cover the fields of innovation, performance management, knowledge management, electronic applications and government communication. The excellent practice category is awarded to the best administrative practice and best technical practice. Finally, the teams category is granted to the best team within government entities.}}
