    about the award

    HH Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai launched on the 24th of June 2009 the Emirates Government Excellence Award under the umbrella of the SKGEP to be the highest award of corporate excellence in UAE, aiming to promote the role of the federal government institutions serving the UAE society at all levels.

The EGEA is divided into 5 different categories and 24 awards to cover the diversity of the work nature of the federal entities and achieve the objectives of SKGEP supporting excellence in all areas. The categories of the EGEA differ in terms of obligatory participation and method of application to the award. Participation is obligatory only in the main categories.
