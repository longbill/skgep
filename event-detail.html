    about the award

    HH Sheikh Mohamed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the UAE, Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai launched on the 24th of June 2009 the Emirates Government Excellence Award under the umbrella of the SKGEP to be the highest award of corporate excellence in UAE, aiming to promote the role of the federal government institutions serving the UAE society at all levels.

Excellence with Sheikh Mohamed Bin Rashid Bin Rashid Bin Rashid

zabeel palace dubai | 6:00PM | sunday
In efforts to recognise Excellence champions, His Highness Shaikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice-President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, received representatives of Government Excellence Programmes from across the country over a Ramadan gathering whereby praised the enormous efforts exerted by Government Excellence Programme members who work as one team to catalyze the progressive march of development of the country.
